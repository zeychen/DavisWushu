About

We are a Chinese martial arts club formed by UC Davis students in 2000 that focuses on endurance, flexibility, and strength.

All levels are welcome

Our club trains practitioners of all skill levels to perform demonstrations for local events and compete in the taolu (forms) portion of annual tournaments, such as Davis Dance Revolution, the Chinese Martial Arts Tournament (CMAT), and the Collegiate Wushu Championships.
